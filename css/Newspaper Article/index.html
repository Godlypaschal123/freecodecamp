Tech Breakthrough Reshapes Urban Living
Smart cities evolve with AI-powered infrastructure
By Chigbo Nwankwo

Urban centers around the world are embracing artificial intelligence to improve transportation, energy efficiency, and public safety. The integration of smart sensors and predictive analytics is transforming how cities respond to daily challenges.

Experts believe this shift will lead to more sustainable environments and better quality of life for residents. However, concerns about data privacy and equitable access remain at the forefront of public discourse.
